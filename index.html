NICE Prime-Factorization Challenge

Mission: The NICE project (Nonlocal Intelligent Consciousness Evidence) seeks to empirically test access to universal intelligence through mathematical tasks. This web app implements the original 20-year-old proposal by Rodriguez and inspiration from Andrew on the DMTx podcast, offering stages from intuitive training to extreme tests of nonlocal retrieval.

NICE Acronym: Nonlocal-Intelligent-Consciousness-Evidence

“All numbers have a unique set of prime factors… but it’s very difficult to do with large numbers.”
— Rodriguez
“...we want to prove your existence. Here’s a very large number. Tell me what the unique prime factors are of this number.”
— Andrew (DMTx Podcast)
